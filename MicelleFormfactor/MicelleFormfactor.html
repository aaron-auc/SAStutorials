Before you start

  • Python or similar for importing and plotting data.

Learning outcomes

  • Learn to build up af form factor from constituents with known form factor amplitudes.
  • Be able to do a Porod plot and understand the interpretation of a Porod plot.
  • Use Kratky plot to assess the foldedness of a particle.
  • For protein, estimate the oligomeric state by determining the molecular weight, M
  • Do an indirect Fourier transformation and retrieve structural parameters, Rg and dmax.
  • Guess and overall shape using the pair distribution (obtained via indirect Fourier transformation).
