Improving the fit

Ideas for complementary experiments
  • The vesicles could be imaged, e.g. with electron microscopy, to get an independent estimation of its structure, number of layers, polydispersity etc.
  • Chemical assays could be used to determine the lipid content.
  • The overall vesicle size may be determined with dynamic light scattering.
Ideas for improving the model
  • The model contains no information about the shape of the vesicle, but instead assumes that the vesicles are infinitely large (radius->inf) - maybe a finite vesicle radius could be built into the model?
  • There may be polydispersity (not all particles are the same) in the multilamellar model (lamellar_hg_stack_caille), e.g., the vesicles may contain different number of layers (polydispersity in the parameter Nlayers)
