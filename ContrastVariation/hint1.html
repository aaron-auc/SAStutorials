Hint

$I(0)$ is proportional to $\Delta\mathrm{SLD}^2 V^2$,

where $\Delta\mathrm{SLD} = \mathrm{SLD}_\mathrm{particle} - \mathrm{SLD}_\mathrm{solvent}$ is the contrast (or, excess scattering length density) and $V$ is the particle volume.

Try, e.g. to simulate a sphere with $\Delta\mathrm{SLD}=0.1$ as Model 1 and a sphere with $\Delta\mathrm{SLD}=10$ as Model 2.
