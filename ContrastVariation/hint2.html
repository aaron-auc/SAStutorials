Solve for X:

SLD_i = SLDD2O

SLD_i = sum(SL)/V_i

sum(SL) = NC * SLC + (NH-X) * SLH + X*SLD + ND*SLD...

be aware of ND, the number of H's that spontaneously exchange with Ds in H2O

    abbreviations:
  • SL: scattering length
  • SLD: scattering length density
  • H,D,C: Hydrogen, Deuterium, Carbon
  • V: volume
