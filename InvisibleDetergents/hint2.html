Solve for $X$: $$SLD_i = SLD_{D2O}$$ $$SLD_i = \frac{\sum{SL}}{V_i}$$ $$\sum{SL} = N_C \cdot SL_C + (N_H-X) \cdot SL_H + X \cdot SL_D + N_D \cdot SL_D$$ Be aware of $N_D$, the number of H's that spontaneously exchange with Ds in H2O

    abbreviations:
  • $SL$: scattering length
  • $SLD$: scattering length density
  • H,D,C: Hydrogen, Deuterium, Carbon
  • $V$: volume
